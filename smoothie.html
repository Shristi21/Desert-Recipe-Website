  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup referigerated banana
  • 1 cup diced pineapple
  • 1 cup baby spinach leaves
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • pinch of black salt
  • pinch of black pepper powder

Directions

  1. Add diced pineapple, banana, one cup milk and spinach leaves into a blender and blend them well so that smooth texture will form.
  2. After that put the smoothie into the glass.
  3. Add 1 tbsp lemon juice, pinch of black salt and pinch of black pepper powder and mix them well.
  4. You can garnish it with cucumber and spinach leaves.

Now as you can see this nutritious and healthy smoothie recipe is ready to present.Enjoy with your family!!
