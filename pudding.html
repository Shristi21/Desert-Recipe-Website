Butterscotch Pudding

Everyone likes to eat desert after their meal.There are various type of deserts which we can have however, we are here with a recipe of butterscotch pudding which is very delicious and easy to make at home. This recipe is also very cheap as everyone can find the ingredients very easily at home and enjoy with it. So,let's begin with the recipe.

Ingredients

Milk

Corn Flour

Sugar

Vanilla Essence

Chocolate Sprinkles

Butter

Previous Next
  • 2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup corn flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Vanilla Essence
  • Chocolate Sprinkles
  • 1/4 cup butter

Directions

  1. Add two cup of milk in a bowl followed with 1/4 cup of corn flour and mix them well.
  2. Now add 1/2 cup of sugar in a pan and caramalised the sugar on medium flame.
  3. Add 1/4 cup butter in caramalised sugar and add the mixture of milk and corn flour.(caramalised sugar will harden while adding milk mixture .
  4. Mix them well and dissolve it on low flame till they get dissolved completely , after that add 1/2 tbsp vanilla essence and stir them well on medium flame until it thickens.
  5. Switch off the flame and pour the mixture in pudding glasses.
  6. Fridge it for 30 minutes and before serving add some chocolate sprinkles over them to garnish the pudding.

You can also add your choice of fruits or dry fruits for garnishing. So, this lusicious pudding recipe is ready to serve and you can relish with your family.Have fun!!
